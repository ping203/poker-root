The number of minutes the tournament is in the "announced" state. During this period, the tournament will not be open for registration. If you want the tournament to open for registration from the moment it is created, set this to 0.
The number of minutes the tournament is open for registration. The tournament will be created (minutes in registration + minutes in announced) minutes before the start date. For example, if the tournament is supposed to start at 18:00, and the minutes in announced is 5 and minutes in registering is 10, the tournament will be created at 17:45, and be in the "announced" state for 5 minutes and then in the "registering" state for 10 minutes.
